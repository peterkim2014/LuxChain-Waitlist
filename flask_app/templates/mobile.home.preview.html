Polkadot Technology

Polkadot's Relay Chain is the central hub that
connects different blockchains ensuring
security and consensus across the network.

Parachains are individual blockchains that run
parallel within the Polkadot network, benefitting
from the Relay Chain's security and
interoperability features.

Together, they form a scalable, decentralized
network that enables different blockchains to
transfer messages and value seamlessly,
fostering a connected and versatile ecosystem.
